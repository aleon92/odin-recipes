Description

This a recipe for deviled eggs. Becareful you might eat the whole batch!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • paprika
  • salt
  • mustard
  • chopped onion
  • Chopped celery

Steps

  1. Get hard boiled eggs ready
  2. Peel hard boiled eggs and cut in half and put the yolk in a bowl
  3. Add in mustard and mayo to yolk and mix
  4. add in chopped onion and celery to mixture
  5. Add salt to taste
  6. Add paprika
  7. Keep refridgerated until ready to server and enjoy!
