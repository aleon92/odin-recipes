Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipe for your basic spaghetti. Hope it does well with all the beginner cooks out there!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Parmasean cheese

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and strain grease
  2. Put ground beef back in skillet and add Spaghetti sauce until warm
  3. In seperate pot bring water to a boil and add noodles
  4. Add noodles to pan with ground beef and spaghetti sauce
  5. Add parmasean cheese to liking
  6. Serve and enjoy
