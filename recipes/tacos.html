Tacos

Description

This is a recipe for the spicy cripsy tacos. They will make you taste buds fill with flavor. Flavor of heat!

Not recommened for people who don't like spicy!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Cripsy taco shells
  • Spicy taco seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Hot sauce
  • Cup of hot water

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and strain grease
  2. Put brownned ground beef back into skillet and pour in water with taco seasoing
  3. Bring to a boil and then simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Cut tomatoes and lettuce to your liking
  5. Heat up taco shells in microwave or oven
  6. Add meat to taco shells and add toppings
  7. Serve and enjoy
